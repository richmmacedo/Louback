Luiza Louback is a Brazilian writer. Her work has appeared or is forthcoming in The Foundationalist, Bridge Ink, Kalopsia Journal, and Parallax Review, among others. She has been recognized by The School of the New York Times, Barnard University, the International Humans Rights Arts Festival, and more. Luiza is an undergraduate student at the University of Pennsylvania majoring in English Literature and Communications with a minor in Journalistic Writing.

When she is not writing, she advocates for literary accessibility in Latin America and gender equality through the non-profit, Lidere-se. For her work as a social entrepreneur, Luiza has been recognized as a Global Teen Leader 2022 and an Ashoka Young Changemaker.
